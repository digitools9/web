What is Digitools?

Digitools is a web-based collection of essential digital utilities developed as our final year college project. It combines 9 tools into a single platform: Image Resizer, Image Format Converter, Image to PDF Converter, PDF Merger, PDF Splitter, PDF Encryptor, PDF Decryptor, QR Code Generator, and URL Shortener.

Why Choose Digitools?

Digitools is fast, simple, and device-independent. It requires no installation, works in the browser, and helps users perform complex file operations with ease. Perfect for students, professionals, and everyday users.
